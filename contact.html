Our Vision

We are a locally owned, family friendly business in down town
Lexington. Our goals is to provide our customers
with high quality food and helpful service. Order out or dine in
for the best health food service in Kentucky.

All our produce is grown locally from organic farms.
We hope to serve our community and give back to those who have
helped us grow. We allow only the highest quality food to be severed.
We take pride in our fresh, healthy food.

Our menu has several vegan and vegetarian friendly options.
We hope to provide healthy options for every taste and diet.
